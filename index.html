Our Weapons

M16 Assault Rifle

The M16 is a lightweight, air-cooled, gas-operated, magazine-fed assault rifle, capable of firing in semi-automatic and fully automatic modes. It is the standard-issue rifle for the United States Armed Forces.

M249 Squad Automatic Weapon (SAW)

The M249 SAW is a light machine gun chambered in 5.56mm NATO. It provides infantry squads with the high rate of fire and suppressive fire capabilities of a machine gun, with the portability and maneuverability of a rifle.
